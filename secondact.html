JOEL.

'Fraid? Bosh!

ANDREW.

I'm afraid to face——

JOEL.

Face what? We won't get caught.

ANDREW.

Your sister—my wife.

ELLEN.

The money was all gone. They turned me out at the old place, and then——

MISSJOYCE.

Sure, I heard the curate reading them to his reverence. He must be a wonder for principles.

MRS DELANE.

Indeed, it is what I was saying to myself, he must be a very saintly young man.

HYACINTH.

Good evening to you. I was bid to come to the post-office——

FARDY.

It would be a great thing to have a character like that.

MRS DELANE.

Indeed, I am thinking it will be long before you will get the like of it, Fardy Farrell.

QUIRKE.

Here is the priest's housekeeper coming.
